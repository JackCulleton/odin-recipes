Blender oreo


Description

Create a 3D Oreo in Blender â€” crisp chocolate cookies with creamy filling, ready to render. A sweet modeling exercise for practicing materials and textures!

Ingredients

  • 1 cylinder mesh (for the cookie base)
  • 1 subdivision surface modifier
  • 1 cookie material (dark brown/black, rough surface)
  • 1 cream filling mesh (another thinner cylinder)
  • 1 cream material (smooth, off-white)
  • Lighting and camera setup for tasty renders

Steps

  1. Open Blender and add a cylinder for the cookie.
  2. Apply a subdivision surface modifier and enable smooth shading.
  3. Create a dark, rough material for the cookie surface.
  4. Add another thin cylinder between two cookies for the cream filling.
  5. Apply a smooth, glossy white material to the cream.
  6. Set up lighting and camera for a photorealistic look.
  7. Render your Oreo and enjoy your 3D snack!
