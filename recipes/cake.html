Description

Bake up a delicious 3D cake in Blender â€” layers of sponge, frosting, and decorations that look good enough to eat, all without leaving your computer!

Ingredients

  • 1 cylinder mesh (for the cake base)
  • 2 or 3 extra cylinders (for layered tiers)
  • 1 subdivision surface modifier (to smooth edges)
  • 1 sponge material (light brown/yellow)
  • 1 frosting material (white, pink, or chocolate)
  • Decorative meshes (spheres for cherries, planes for candles, etc.)
  • Lighting and camera setup for the final render

Steps

  1. Open Blender and add a cylinder as your cake base.
  2. Scale the cylinder to look like a cake layer.
  3. Duplicate and stack cylinders to create multiple tiers.
  4. Apply subdivision surface and smooth shading for soft, rounded edges.
  5. Assign a sponge material to the inner part of the cake.
  6. Add a frosting material to the top and sides (use a separate mesh or texture).
  7. Place decorations like cherries, sprinkles, or candles on top.
  8. Set up lighting and camera for a warm, tasty render.
  9. Render your cake masterpiece and enjoy your digital dessert!
